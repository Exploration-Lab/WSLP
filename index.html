IJCNLP-AACL 2025 SLT4LRL Workshop

Sign Language Translation for Low-Resource Languages

Co-located with IJCNLP-AACL 2025 in Abu Dhabi, UAE on November 20th, 2025
Shared Task Important Dates

Over 430 million people worldwide have disabling hearing loss, and sign languages are essential for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community. In India, with 63 million DHH individuals, Indian Sign Language (ISL) is widely used but lacks technological support. This workshop advances AI and NLP for sign language processing, focusing on low-resource languages like ISL, to improve accessibility in education, healthcare, and public services.

Join our shared tasks and help build inclusive technologies for sign language users.
